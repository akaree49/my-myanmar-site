Why I love these places

Each picture here means something to me; maybe it’s a place I’ve been with family, maybe it’s just somewhere I dream of going again. Either way, I hope you see a bit of the magic too.

Bagan at sunrise

Watching hot air balloons drift over ancient temples is honestly something you never forget.

Inle Lake life

Floating gardens, fishermen with crazy balance, and the calmest water.

Shwedagon at night

Always lit up with candles and prayers that somehow feels both lively and peaceful at the same time.

Street food moments

Stopping by random stalls for noodles or tea is half the fun (and always worth it).

Thanks for checking it out!
